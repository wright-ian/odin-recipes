Beer Can Chicken

Description

This recipe for beer can chicken will yield four delicious servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 12-ounce can of beer, room temperature, opened, half-full
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
