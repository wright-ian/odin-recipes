Beer Can Chicken

Description

This recipe for beer can chicken will yield four delicious servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 12-ounce can of beer, room temperature, opened, half-full
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper

Directions

  1. Heat up half of the grill
  2. Remove neck and giblets from cavity of chicken. Mix salt, pepper, and thyme, and rub all over chicken along with olive oil.
  3. Lower the chicken onto the beer cah so the chicken is sitting upright with the can inside.
  4. Place the chicken on the cool side of the grill. Use the legs and beer can as a tripod to support the chicken. Cover the grill and leave it for at least half an hour. Check every 15 minutes thereafter. Cook until the internal temperature of the thickest part of the thigh reaches 165 degrees F.
  5. Transfer the chicken to a tray and let rest for 10 minutes. Be careful when removing the can as it will still be hot, and full.
